# Extended Naturalism

## Standard Naturalism

There is a performative contradiction in (i.e. one cannot perform the action of) living a meaningful life with your spouse while upholding a worldview in which everything is just atoms in the void. In Standard Naturalism, in the current standard scientific worldview, love is nothing but a chemical reaction, love is not real. We are just a bunch of atoms bouncing off one another in a cold, meaningless container.
